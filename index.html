Welcome to the Orchard Keepers

Under the shade of a giant Sequoia tree, nestled in a beautiful established garden surrounded by neighboring vineyards and orchards you will find our home. From our family to yours we invite you to come and enjoy Orchard Keepers. Originally 3 separate cottages dating back to the 1890′s & 1950′s, Orchard Keepers has now been lovingly restored and transformed into a beautiful four bedroom home & separate Workers Cottage providing country chic accommodation for 10 people on 10 acres  in the heart of Red Hill
