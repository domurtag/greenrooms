  • Matthew

Matthew has a wealth of experience in creating landscapes and public spaces and in the design of studios, workspaces, homes and extensions. An awareness of the primary importance of the natural world and our connection with it informs Matthew's work.

Matthew has studied Horticulture and Landscape Architecture and holds a BA in Mental and Moral Science from Trinity College Dublin. He is a member of the Environmental Design Research Association.

  • Louise

Louise is an artist who has worked extensively in both interior and exterior design. Her natural flair and artistic ability ensure that the spaces she creates have a deep resonance. Louise's very keen sense of what is necessary, what is nice and what is irrelevant allows her to create rich, vibrant spaces that have enhanced many lives.

Louise studied Fine Art and holds a BA in Art History and Sociology from University College Dublin.
